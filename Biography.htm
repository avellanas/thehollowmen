Biography

Many excellent biographies of T.S. Eliot have already, so I feel no need to recap his entire life story ad nausea. Instead I've included a few websites that do a perfectly adequate job of presenting Eliot's biography.

http://www.deathclock.com/thunder/timeline.html
A timeline of Eliot's life. Also links to other Eliot resources.

http://people.a2000.nl/avanarum/index.html
Detailed timeline. Only goes up through 1922.

Gordon, Lyndall T.S. Eliot : an imperfect life New York : W.W. Norton & Co., 1999
Recent biography that emphasizes some of Eliot's less savory characteristics. Also see Gordon's Eliot's Early Years and Eliot's New Life.
